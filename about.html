Events such as Bible studies, symposiums, public service events and other student-centered activities are regularly conducted in various chapters to emphasize the importance of Bible reading to the youth.











MISSION



To encourage people, especially in the academic and professional realms, in reading the Bible and instilling Christian values that are written therein.
